Description

A salad is a dish consisting of a combination of raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, grains, nuts, seeds, and sometimes meats or seafood, typically served cold. It can be dressed with a variety of seasonings, such as vinaigrette, ranch, or Caesar dressing, to enhance its flavor.

The vibrant colors and textures of the various ingredients in a salad make it a visually appealing and healthy meal option. Depending on the ingredients used, a salad can be light and refreshing or hearty and filling, making it a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oi
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Salad

  • 2 (5 ounce) packages American salad mix
  • 3/4 cup pitted black olives
  • 3/4 cup pickled pepperoncini
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 2 plum tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 (4 1/2 ounce) package large seasoned croutons

Steps

  1. Combine olive oil, mayonnaise, vinegar, lemon juice, sugar, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, garlic, and Parmesan in a food processor.
  2. Process until thoroughly combined.
  3. Combine salad mix, olives, pepperoncini, onion, tomatoes, and croutons in a large bowl.
  4. Drizzle salad with dressing; toss to coat.
  5. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
